I'm a UX/UI Designer located in Phoenix, AZ. Who is currently working as a freelancer but is seeking full-time work opportunities. I incorporate my training in gestalt theory, heuristics, cognitive bias, assessability, and the double diamond method to provide a strong foundation when developing a user experience.

As a strategist of experience, I strive for balance in my work and personal life. In addition, I'm an explorer of knowledge who enjoys looking into the thought process of how people interact within the world. My keen empathic sense of the human condition is likely my greatest strength. My interests include true crime, video games, music, art, psychology, sustainability, technology, and stumbling upon memorable experiences.
