Wise Pantry

Redesign of the online permit application websites of three departments of Maricopa County.

Project Overview

A service that helps find recipes you can make using the ingredients you have at home. The app is to targets people who are cooking from home to help them utilize all the food in the pantry. The app will help keep track of what you have in the pantry and what you have already used, then find recipes with what is in the pantry. The app could be linked to the grocery ordering app and has a food item's scan ability once it is used.

Empathize

Process
  • -Talked to potential users to gain insight into ingredients to recipe apps
  • -Looked at 4 apps that have useful functions for ingredients to recipe apps
  • -User reviews on the 4 similar apps to gain useful insight

Interview Takeawayss
  • Joe:"I always have ingredients left in my cupboard that I don't know what to do with."
  • Danielle:" Taking a picture of the items you would want to use would be beneficial if no barcode is not available to scan."
  • Aaron and Sydney:"I am so tired of scrolling through someone's life story to get to a recipe."
  • Britt: " I think some useful functions would be to input not only the items you have but filter results based on the type of dish and taste preference (spicy)"

Competitive Analysis
  • -Manage your pantry using your voice.
  • -Shopping List that talks to your pantry.
  • -Filters and Diets to discover dishes.

Define


User Insights
  • -Most users want to be able to add ingredients and find recipes quickly
  • -Having preference filters helps cater to dishes that the user wants to see in the results.
  • -Having intuitive functions to help with items that are in the users' pantry when it deals with quantity and converting measurements of ingredients in recipes.

Ideate

List of ideas gathered from user interviews and competitive analysis that would be helpful in the functionality of the app.


Ideas
  • -User can quickly use the camera to add ingredients to the pantry.
  • -The ability to add ingredients to a shopping list from a recommended recipes.
  • -Have the ability to list allergies, food sensitivities, likes, dislikes, and cuisines preferences to filter unwanted recipes.
  • -Home screen will have the option to look at ways to save food as well as add favorite recipes.
  • -The recipes will be internal instead of outsourcing links. The will allow the users to get the instructions and ingredients quickly. It will also help with intuitively adding ingredients you need to the shopping list. .

Testing

The users were first tasked to add Dietary Preferences. Then they were asked to add ingredients to the pantry with the camera feature; after that, they were to take those ingredients to the pantry list. Once in the pantry, the users were to look at recipes and add missing ingredients to the shopping list that is then taken to the shopping cart and check out process.

Main takeaways :
  • -The app should have friendly descriptions of why you need allergies and food preference information.
  • -Is that most users want simplicity in adding the ingredient function.
  • -Each page should be able to guide users with helpful descriptions and tips on how the app works.
  • -The more intuitive, the better the overall confusion level will be in adding ingredients to the pantry.
  • -It should be quick to add ingredients with a camera and manual typing.
  • -The bubbles with ingredients made the users assume that they already had these ingredients added because the bubble had a small x on the right-hand side.
  • -Some of the buttons were too far from the navbar.
  • -The ingredients should also list the quantity of what you have in the pantry, what you will add to the pantry, and what will be added to your cart in the shopping list feature.
